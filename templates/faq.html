The function of this tool is to empower any disabled person to gain more control and understanding of what their rights are as employees in the modern workforce as well as to allow employers to equip themselves with the necessary tools to support disabled employees.

Each workplace can vary in terms of the location and accessibility however we want to help and empower employers to create an equal opportunity workplace for all staff.
